Asset Tagging Portal — Case Study

Project Overview

The Asset Tagging Portal is a large-scale asset management system developed for vendors working with Pepsi Pakistan. The platform enables nationwide tagging, verification, and tracking of company-owned assets — primarily vehicles and beverage coolers — distributed across Pakistan.

Business Context

Pepsi operates thousands of physical assets deployed with vendors, distributors, and retail partners across multiple cities. Accurate tagging and visibility of these assets is critical for ownership verification, operational audits, and loss prevention.

Problem

Prior to this system, asset tracking was fragmented and unreliable:

  • Manual or semi-digital asset records maintained by different vendors
  • No centralized system to verify asset ownership and location
  • Delayed synchronization of field data into the main database
  • Performance issues when handling large volumes of asset data
  • No real-time visibility into tagged vs untagged assets

Solution

I helped in developed a centralized Asset Tagging Portal that allows Pepsi vendors to digitally tag, verify, and update asset information in the field. The system was designed to handle high-volume data ingestion using asynchronous processing via Laravel Queues.

Asset Types Managed

  • Vehicles – Delivery trucks and operational vehicles tagged with unique identifiers and metadata.
  • Coolers – Refrigeration units deployed at retail locations, tracked by serial numbers and location data.

Core Modules

  • Asset Tagging – Digital tagging of assets with unique IDs, images, and metadata captured by vendors.
  • Vendor Management – Controlled access for vendors and field teams to tag and manage assigned assets.
  • Asset Verification – Validation workflows to ensure data accuracy and prevent duplicate or invalid entries.
  • Bulk Data Processing – High-volume asset uploads and updates processed asynchronously using Laravel Queues.
  • Reporting & Dashboards – Visibility into total assets, tagged vs untagged counts, regional distribution, and vendor performance.

Scalability & Performance

  • Processed large-scale asset data across multiple regions in Pakistan
  • Used Laravel Queues to offload heavy operations and avoid request timeouts
  • Ensured reliable background processing for uploads, validations, and sync jobs
  • Designed the system to scale with increasing asset volume and vendor usage

Tech Stack

  • Laravel (Backend & Queue Processing)
  • MySQL (High-volume relational data storage)
  • Queue Workers (Asynchronous job handling)
  • Bootstrap / Admin UI (Vendor-facing portal)

My Responsibilities

  • Queue-based processing for large datasets
  • Asset tagging and verification workflows
  • Performance optimization for high-load operations
  • Vendor access control and data integrity enforcement

Impact

  • Centralized asset visibility across Pakistan
  • Accurate and auditable asset records
  • Improved operational control for Pepsi vendors
  • Eliminated manual reconciliation of asset data
  • Stable performance despite large-scale data volume

Conclusion

The Asset Tagging Portal provided Pepsi Pakistan with a reliable, scalable solution to track and manage physical assets nationwide, while enabling vendors to efficiently tag and verify assets without overwhelming the system.

Report Ordering System — Case Study

Project Overview

The Report Ordering System is a centralized platform designed for individuals, banks, and financial institutions to request, track, bill, and analyze professional financial and credit-related reports. The system digitizes the entire lifecycle — from report request to delivery, billing, and performance analytics.

Problem

Banks and individuals traditionally rely on emails, phone calls, and manual follow-ups to request business and credit reports. This creates operational inefficiencies and risks:

  • No standardized report request process
  • Lack of transparency on report status and turnaround time
  • Manual billing and invoice mismatches
  • No consolidated reporting statistics for management
  • Difficulty handling high-volume institutional orders

Solution

I developed a web-based Report Ordering System that allows users to place structured report orders, track progress in real time, automate billing, and generate operational statistics. The system supports both individual customers and institutional clients such as banks.

Supported Report Types

  • Business Information Reports (BIR) – Company background, ownership structure, financial standing, and operational insights.
  • International Credit Reports – Cross-border creditworthiness reports sourced through international partners.
  • Local Credit Reports – Domestic credit checks tailored to local financial and regulatory requirements.
  • Income Estimation Reports – Professional income assessments used for lending, risk evaluation, and compliance.

Core System Modules

  • Report Ordering – Structured order forms with report type selection, priority level, and required documentation.
  • Order Tracking – Real-time status tracking (Requested, In Progress, Completed, Delivered).
  • Billing & Invoicing – Automated invoice generation per report or bulk orders, with bank-specific pricing rules.
  • Statistics & Analytics – Dashboards showing total orders, report type distribution, revenue trends, turnaround times, and client-wise performance.

Additional Features

  • Individual & Bank Client Management
  • Bulk Ordering for Financial Institutions
  • PDF Report Upload & Secure Delivery
  • User Roles & Access Control (Admin, Analyst, Billing, Client)
  • Audit Logs and Order History

Tech Stack

  • Laravel (Backend & Business Logic)
  • Vue.js (Frontend Interface)
  • MySQL (Relational Database)
  • Bootstrap (Responsive UI)

My Responsibilities

  • System architecture and database design
  • End-to-end order workflow implementation
  • Billing logic and invoice automation
  • Statistics dashboards and reporting
  • Role-based access and security controls

Impact

  • Standardized and traceable report ordering process
  • Reduced manual coordination and follow-ups
  • Accurate, automated billing for banks and individuals
  • Clear visibility into report volumes and revenue
  • Scalable system capable of handling institutional demand

Conclusion

The Report Ordering System replaced fragmented manual processes with a secure, scalable, and transparent platform, enabling financial institutions and individuals to request, track, and manage professional reports efficiently.

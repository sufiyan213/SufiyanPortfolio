Prism Surveyors ERP — Case Study

Project Overview

Prism Surveyors ERP is a specialized insurance management platform designed for surveyors who prepare inspection reports, loss assessments, and insurance-related documentation. The system centralizes insurance reporting, automates invoicing, and provides real-time business statistics.

Problem

Insurance surveyors face slow, manual workflows — preparing reports in Word/PDF, tracking jobs in spreadsheets, handling billing manually, and lacking visibility into workload and revenue. This results in:

  • Frequent report errors and inconsistent formats
  • No automated link between jobs and invoices
  • Difficulty tracking pending, completed, and overdue cases
  • No centralized statistics for monthly workload or revenue
  • Scattered client and insurance company data

Solution

I built Prism Surveyors ERP as a complete digital system that automates report creation, connects jobs with invoice generation, and provides dashboards for statistics. The system ensures accuracy, consistency, and real-time visibility across insurance operations.

Core Modules

  • Insurance Reporting – Create and manage Motor Survey Reports, Fire Reports and Loss Assessments with structured templates and standardized formats.
  • Invoicing – Auto-generate invoices based on completed reports, apply company-specific billing rules, manage due payments, and maintain invoice history.
  • Statistics & Analytics – Dashboard showing monthly workload, completed jobs, revenue totals, top clients, insurance company breakdowns, and pending case alerts.

Additional Features

  • Client & Insurance Company Management
  • Job Assignment and Status Tracking
  • PDF Report Generation
  • User Roles & Permissions (Admin, Surveyor, Billing, Viewer)
  • Activity Logs and Audit Trails

Tech Stack

  • Laravel 7 (Backend API)
  • Vue.js 2 (Frontend UI)
  • MySQL (Database)
  • Bootstrap 4 (Layout & Forms)

My Responsibilities

  • Backend architecture and API development
  • Frontend UI development in Vue.js
  • Report templates and PDF generation
  • Invoice automation logic
  • Dashboard and analytics implementation
  • Database design and optimization

Impact

  • Report preparation time reduced by over 50%
  • Billing accuracy improved through automated invoice rules
  • Full visibility of daily, weekly, and monthly workload
  • Centralized system eliminated scattered spreadsheets
  • Improved operational efficiency for survey teams

Conclusion

Prism Surveyors ERP modernized the insurance reporting workflow, giving surveyors a reliable and scalable tool for managing inspection reports, billing, and performance analytics in one platform.
